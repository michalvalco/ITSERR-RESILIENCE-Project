## How to Use This Log

At the end of each day (or morning of the next), complete the template below for that day. This creates a narrative record of your fellowship journey, supporting:

- Progress tracking against milestones
- Reflection on research direction
- Documentation of decisions and pivots
- Material for final reports and future papers
