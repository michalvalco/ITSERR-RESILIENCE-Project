## Structural Elements

### Lemma Pattern

Stöckel's commentary structure typically follows this pattern:

1. **Lemma introduction:** A term or concept from Melanchthon's Loci
2. **Definition:** `Est autem...` or similar definitional phrase
3. **Scriptural support:** `Sicut Paulus...`, `Hinc...`, biblical citations
4. **Patristic confirmation:** References to Church Fathers
5. **Conclusion:** `Quare...`, `Itaque...`, application

- **Chapter headers:** ALL CAPS titles (DE PECCATO, DE LEGE, etc.)
